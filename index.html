--Applied Term--
James-Lange theory

--Term Explanation--
The James-Lange theory focuses on how stimuli preceed emotions
and actually cause the emotions to happen. This song, although
the lyrics are irrelevant, tends to cause goosebumps and an
increased heart rate in most people (according to my observations)
This means that the listener begins to feel afraid and is now in fear
which exactly supports the James-Lange theory.

Learned Helplessness

--Term Explanation--
Learned Helplessness refers to the point in which you
decide that pretty much everything you can do to affect
a situation cannot and will not affect the end result.
Basically, you admit that you are helpless to stop the result
and that the end is unstoppable. The first thing I thought of
was, you guessed it, love. We all know we can't stop loving that
one person sometimes, no matter how hard we try. This song illustrates
that perfectly.
